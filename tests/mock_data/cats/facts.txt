Cats have been domesticated for over 4,000 years.
The average cat sleeps 12-16 hours per day.
Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
A group of cats is called a clowder.
Cats have five toes on their front paws but only four on their back paws.
The world's oldest cat lived to be 38 years old.
Cats can jump up to six times their length.
Cats have a special reflective layer in their eyes called the tapetum lucidum.
Cats have over 100 vocal sounds, while dogs only have about 10.
Cats spend about 30% of their waking hours grooming themselves.
